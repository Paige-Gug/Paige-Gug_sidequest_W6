## Description

This p5.js project known as “Bread and Balance” has been designed as a cozy game for players to learn how to work in a bakery. The player is looking to save up enough money for culinary school. You are given pacing activities and must keep a balance throughout your day, looking at various tradeoffs. For example, balancing how long you keep your shop open vs. getting the most money. If you work too much you may have to take the day off. Players will notice their energy level will often decrease through cognitive tasks with randomized challenges.

The game currently has one level as this is the first phase and testing experience. This level introduces players to the storyline where they are given a sourdough starter, the ability to find ingredients, drag them to the bowl, bake the bread and complete the task.

## Interaction Instructions

Users will notice the homescreen indicates they have the opportunity to view pantry, workbench, and oven functionality. These buttons will highlight to indicate the mouse has hovered over and buttons can be clicked to take you to your respective choice.
